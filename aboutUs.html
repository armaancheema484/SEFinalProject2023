Welcome to Weather by Armaan, your one-stop shop for all things weather.

I am an individual with a passion for programming and am also a weather enthusiast. This makes me committed to providing you with the most accurate and up-to-date weather information possible. I am currently still regarding this website as a work in progress, but I had a lot of fun learning new things and playing around with the setup of my website. I plan to keep working and improving this project and hopefully reach new heights with it one day...

    This website offers a variety of weather products and services, including:

  • Hourly forecast: Get the latest forecast for your location, including temperature, feels-like temperature, rain and wind.

  • Weekly forecast: Plan your future trips with our long-range forecasts, which covers the entire upcoming week.

  • Weather radar: See current and forecast weather conditions on our weather maps.

  • Weather alerts: Receive real-time weather alerts for your area, so you can stay safe in severe weather.

We are committed to providing you with the most accurate and up-to-date weather information possible. Our data is sourced from a variety of reliable sources, including the National Weather Service, NOAA, and AccuWeather. While we lack a team of experienced meteorologists, unlike most other reputable weather services, we are always on the lookout for the latest weather trends. We use this information to create our forecasts, which are updated every hour. I believe that everyone should have access to accurate weather information. That's why I this service for free. I am also working on developing a mobile app that you can download to get weather information on the go.

I hope you enjoy using this website. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact me.

Features for the future:

  1. A radar map: This shows you the current radar conditions in your area.
  2. Display weather in selectable location: added support besides pulling data from geolocation.
  3. A 10-day forecast: This gives you a more detailed look at the weather for the next 10 days.
  4. A severe weather alert system: This will notify you if there is a severe weather event in your area.

Thank you for choosing Weather by Armaan!
